Our Story

 

The name Viane came from one of Hayley Jackson’s favourite movies, Chocolat. Inspired by the main character’s zest for life boldness to live outside the norm, Hayley Jackson always had an abundance of style in her life and home. She also kept her home inviting and peaceful through modern elements and antiques. Creating a home that you love and that reflects you and your family is what we strive for. It doesn’t have to be the most on-trend style or cutting edge design, it just has to be you. Hayley grew up on Salt Spring Island and was exposed to the eclectic stylings of many homes and lives. She studied at Pacific Design Academy in 2018. In 2019 she trained with Maria Killam and is a certified true colour expert. She then worked for the last few years at Hook and Hook designs in Sidney as their junior designer and project management assistant. Viane Designs & Styling is here to make your home your happy place!
